History of TechCon

TechCon started as a small local gathering in 2010 and has evolved into one of the fastest leading technology conferences globally. Thousands of attendees come together to share ideas and innovations yearly.

Our Mission

The mission of TechCon is to empower Professionals, innovators, and students to embrace technology. We provide a platform where future tech leaders can learn, collaborate, and drive impactful innovation.

Notable Past Speakers

We have hosted some of the most influential minds in tech over the years.

  • Dr. Ada Lovelace: AI researcher and visionary in ethical machine learning.

  • Grace Hopper: She developed the first compiler that made programming accessible.
